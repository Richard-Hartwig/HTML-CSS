Mission Statement

Hardware R Us is a value-driven company run by a highly motivated crew of people whose goal is to understand the costumer’s needs so that they can provide a remarkable costumer service experience. Satisfaction is very important to us, that is why we will always do our best to adapt and provide our costumer with the best products.


History

The Hardware R Us story began in South Florida when Author Grimes, part owner of the North Hardware Store Company, envisioned creating a chain of hardware stores. Sales grew over time, and Hardware R Us stores opened in neighboring towns throughout South Florida.
